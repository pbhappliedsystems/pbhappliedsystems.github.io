2. Information We Collect

PBH Applied Systems, LLC collects or processes information in connection with the following activities:

  • AI Agent Demo: When you use the AI Agent Demo on our website, we collect the prompts you submit, session identifiers, and — if you provide it — your email address to unlock additional queries.
  • Email gate: If you choose to submit your email address to continue using the AI Agent Demo beyond the free query limit, we collect and store a cryptographic hash of that email address. The raw email address is used only to send a one-time follow-up message and is not stored in plaintext.
  • Usage data: We log which models were used, query counts per session, estimated GPU compute cost, and response timing. No prompt content is retained after the session ends.
  • Consulting engagements: Project-related communications, scoping documents, and deliverable materials shared in the course of a client engagement.
  • Website interactions: Standard server logs, referring URLs, and anonymized usage data that may be collected by our hosting provider.
  • Content distribution tools: Tools that interact with third-party platform APIs (YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook) on behalf of our own business accounts — including platform account identifiers, OAuth tokens, content metadata, and basic analytics.

3. AI Agent Demo — Data Handling

The AI Agent Demo at pbhappliedsystems.com/assistant.html allows visitors to interact with quantized language models evaluated by PBH Applied Systems. The following describes how data flows through this system:

What happens to your prompt: Your prompt is transmitted over HTTPS to our API backend, forwarded to a GPU inference container running on Modal.com's infrastructure, and returned as a response. Prompt content is not stored after inference completes.
Session tokens: A random session identifier is stored in your browser's local storage to track your query count within a session. This token contains no personal information and is not linked to your identity unless you provide an email address.
Email address (optional): If you submit your email address to unlock additional queries, we store a one-way cryptographic hash of that address in our session database. Your raw email address is used only to send a single follow-up message from PBH Applied Systems describing our quantized model evaluation services. You may opt out of further contact at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in that message or by emailing admin@pbhappliedsystems.com.
GPU compute: Inference runs on Modal.com's serverless GPU infrastructure. Prompt data is transmitted to Modal's servers solely for the purpose of generating your response. Modal's privacy policy governs how data is handled on their infrastructure: modal.com/privacy.

We do not sell, rent, share, or license any information collected through the AI Agent Demo to third parties.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected through our tools and services is used exclusively to:

  • Operate the AI Agent Demo and manage query session limits
  • Send a single follow-up email to prospects who provide their email address through the demo gate

  • Improve the demo and evaluate model performance over time using anonymized session metrics
  • Automate the scheduling and publication of pre-produced educational content to our own social media accounts
  • Deliver consulting services and technical engagements to clients
  • Develop and distribute educational AI/ML content and curriculum

We do not sell, rent, share, or license any information accessed through these systems to third parties.

6. Data Storage and Security

Session data and hashed email addresses are stored in a SQLite database hosted on Railway's infrastructure within the AI Agent Demo backend. API credentials and access tokens are stored as environment variables and are never committed to source control or transmitted outside their intended service endpoints.

We implement reasonable technical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data handled, including HTTPS-only transmission, access controls, hashed storage of email addresses, and environment variable isolation for all credentials.

7. Data Retention

Session records are retained for up to 90 days and then deleted. Hashed email addresses associated with the demo email gate are retained until an unsubscribe request is received or until the demo is discontinued. OAuth tokens for content distribution tools are retained only as long as necessary for automated publishing and are revoked when no longer needed. Client engagement materials are retained in accordance with applicable professional and legal obligations.
