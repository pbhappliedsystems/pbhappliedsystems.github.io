This Privacy Policy explains how PBH Applied Systems, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with all services, products, tools, and systems produced or operated by PBH Applied Systems, LLC. We are committed to transparency and responsible data handling.

1. Who We Are

PBH Applied Systems, LLC is a sole-member limited liability company engaged in applied AI and machine learning research, education, content production, and technical consulting. Our services include the Scalable Media Infrastructure (SMI) — a system for producing and distributing educational video content — as well as AI/ML engineering services, consulting engagements, and technical educational content development.

Contact: admin@pbhappliedsystems.com

2. Information We Collect

PBH Applied Systems, LLC may collect or process information in connection with the following activities:

  • Content distribution tools that interact with third-party platform APIs (YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook) on behalf of our own business accounts
  • Consulting and client engagements — including project-related communications, scoping documents, and deliverable materials shared in the course of an engagement
  • Website interactions — including standard server logs, referring URLs, and anonymized usage data that may be collected by our hosting provider

Specifically, our content distribution tools may access or process: platform account identifiers, OAuth access tokens, content metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, upload timestamps), and basic analytics data returned by platform APIs for our own published content.

We do not collect personal information from third-party users or viewers of our content through these tools.

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed through our tools and services is used exclusively to:

  • Automate the scheduling and publication of pre-produced educational video content to our own social media accounts
  • Track the status of published content for internal production logging
  • Monitor performance analytics for our own published content
  • Maintain and refresh API credentials required for automated posting
  • Deliver consulting services and technical engagements to clients
  • Develop and distribute educational AI/ML content and curriculum

We do not sell, rent, share, or license any information accessed through these systems to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

API credentials and access tokens are stored locally on secured systems owned and operated by PBH Applied Systems, LLC. We do not store credentials in publicly accessible repositories or transmit them to third-party services outside of the intended platform API endpoints.

We implement reasonable technical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data handled, including access controls and environment variable isolation for credential storage.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as necessary to support automated publishing operations. Tokens are revoked and deleted when no longer needed or when access is terminated. Production logs are retained for internal auditing and may be deleted at our discretion. Client engagement materials are retained in accordance with applicable professional and legal obligations.
